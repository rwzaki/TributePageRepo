Biography
Ayoob Tarish Absi is a Yemeni singer and musician from the area of Al-Aboos in the Ta'izz Governorate. He brought to the world a spectacularly wonderful and colorful portrait of traditional Yemeni songs and music. His songs accompanied us during happy and sad days. His patriotism was clearly resembled in the national anthem and other national works he contributed to the country.
